5. Elbow Method

In[11]:
inertias = []
for k in range(1, 11):
    kmeans = KMeans(n_clusters=k, random_state=42)
    kmeans.fit(X)
    inertias.append(kmeans.inertia_)

plt.figure(figsize=(8,6))
plt.plot(range(1, 11), inertias, marker='o', linestyle='--')
plt.xlabel("Number of Clusters")
plt.ylabel("Inertia")
plt.title("Determining the Optimum Number of Clusters with Elbow Method")
plt.savefig('/content/drive/MyDrive/Colab Notebooks/elbow_plot.png', dpi=300)
plt.show()

6. KMeans Model

In[12]:
kmeans = KMeans(n_clusters=5, init='k-means++', random_state=42)
y_kmeans = kmeans.fit_predict(X)
df['Cluster'] = y_kmeans

plt.figure(figsize=(10, 8))
sns.scatterplot(data=df, x='Annual Income (k$)', y='Spending Score (1-100)', hue='Cluster', palette='Set2', s=70)
plt.title('Customer Segmentation by KMeans')

Based on the clustering results using KMeans with 2 features (Annual Income and Spending Score), we identified 5 distinct customer segments. Below is a summary of each cluster and potential marketing strategies:

Cluster 0 High Income, Low Spending

  • Characteristics: Customers with high annual income but low spending scores.
  • Strategy: Promote exclusive or premium products, personalized offers to encourage spending.

Cluster 1 Low Income, High Spending

  • Characteristics: Customers with relatively low income but high spending behavior.
  • Strategy: Focus on loyalty programs, cashback, and student-friendly promotions.

Cluster 2 Moderate Income & Spending

  • Characteristics: Customers with average income and moderate spending.
  • Strategy: General marketing campaigns, bundled offers to increase average order value.

Cluster 3 High Income, High Spending

  • Characteristics: Wealthy customers with a strong tendency to spend.
  • Strategy: Key target for premium products, VIP memberships, and personalized services.

Cluster 4 Low Income, Low Spending

  • Characteristics: Customers with both low income and low spending.
  • Strategy: Focus less marketing here, or apply low-risk engagement such as social media retargeting.

Conclusion: This segmentation enables the business to design targeted marketing strategies tailored to each group. It helps maximize return on investment (ROI) and enhances customer experience by understanding behavioral patterns.
